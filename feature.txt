The idea is to handle many labelled columns from CSV files and provide filtering on them.


Sample CSV :

      date	   AM      PM
2010-10-18	00h30	04h00
2010-10-19	00h30	07h30
2010-10-20	00h30	00h00
2010-10-26	00h00	00h30


Sample commands :

prompt$> ./TimeSheet.py file.csv before 2010-10-20 AM
(1, 30)

prompt$> ./TimeSheet.py file.csv before 2010-10-20 AM,PM
(1, 30), (12, 0)

prompt$> ./TimeSheet.py file.csv before 2010-10-20 AM+PM
(13, 30)
